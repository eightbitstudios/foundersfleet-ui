Overview

FoundersFleet offers SMS notifications to keep you connected with your squad activities, meeting reminders, and important updates. By opting in to receive SMS messages, you agree to these terms.

Opt-In Agreement

By providing your mobile phone number and opting in to SMS notifications, you agree to receive:

  • Squad meeting reminders
  • Important squad activity notifications
  • Mission deadline reminders
  • Platform updates and announcements
  • Account security alerts

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your squad activity and preferences. On average, users receive 2-5 messages per week. You can customize your notification preferences in your account settings.

Message & Data Rates

Standard message and data rates may apply according to your mobile plan. FoundersFleet does not charge for SMS messages, but your carrier may charge you for receiving texts. Please contact your mobile carrier for details about your plan.

How to Opt-Out

You can opt-out of SMS messages at any time by:

  • Texting STOP to any message you receive from us
  • Updating your notification preferences in account settings
  • Contacting support at support@foundersfleet.com

After opting out, you will receive a final confirmation message and no further SMS messages will be sent.

Help & Support

For help with SMS notifications:

  • Text HELP to receive support information
  • Email support@foundersfleet.com
  • Visit your account settings to manage preferences
