Community Guidelines

Build together. Win together.

Last updated: January 31, 2025

FoundersFleet is built on the belief that founders succeed together. Our community thrives when members support each other with honesty, respect, and genuine commitment to collective growth. These guidelines help maintain the high-trust environment essential for meaningful founder collaboration.

Core Values

Radical Accountability

Show up for your squad. Honor your commitments. When you join a squad, you're making a promise to 4 other founders counting on your participation and insights.

Confidentiality First

What's shared in squad stays in squad. Respect the trust your squad members place in you when they share challenges, metrics, and strategies.

Constructive Candor

Give honest feedback with good intentions. Challenge ideas, not people. The best squads balance support with the tough questions that drive real progress.

Pay It Forward

Share your knowledge generously. Today's advice receiver is tomorrow's advice giver. The community grows stronger when everyone contributes.

Squad Participation Guidelines

Meeting Attendance

  • Attend weekly squad meetings consistently
  • Notify your squad in advance if you can't make it
  • Come prepared with updates and questions
  • Be present and engaged (cameras on, distractions off)

Meeting Conduct

  • Respect time limits and let everyone share
  • Listen actively before offering advice
  • Ask permission before giving unsolicited feedback
  • Keep discussions focused on founder challenges

Between Meetings

  • Update your progress on missions regularly
  • Respond to squad messages within 48 hours
  • Share wins and learnings with your squad
  • Offer help when squad members face challenges

Prohibited Behavior

The following behaviors will result in warnings or removal from FoundersFleet:

Harassment or Discrimination

Any form of harassment, discrimination, or hate speech based on race, gender, religion, sexual orientation, or other characteristics.

Aggressive Solicitation

Spamming squad members with sales pitches, MLM schemes, or unrelated business opportunities.

Dishonesty

Misrepresenting your experience, metrics, or startup status. Authenticity builds trust.

Confidentiality Breaches

Sharing squad members' private information, strategies, or struggles outside the squad without permission.

Consistent No-Shows

Repeatedly missing meetings without notice or valid reason undermines squad cohesion.

Best Practices for Success

Give Great Updates

  • Share specific metrics and progress
  • Be honest about struggles
  • Ask clear, actionable questions
  • Follow up on previous commitments

Provide Valuable Feedback

  • Share relevant experiences
  • Offer specific suggestions
  • Connect members to resources
  • Challenge assumptions respectfully

Build Squad Culture

  • Celebrate squad members' wins
  • Check in during tough times
  • Share relevant opportunities
  • Create psychological safety

Use Platform Features

  • Update mission progress regularly
  • Complete your objectives
  • Share updates in squad feed
  • Suggest new missions/objectives

Enforcement

We take these guidelines seriously to maintain a high-quality community. Violations are handled as follows:

1

First Violation: Warning

Private message explaining the issue and expected behavior

2

Second Violation: Temporary Suspension

1-2 week suspension from squad activities

3

Third Violation: Permanent Removal

Account termination and removal from all squads

Severe violations (harassment, fraud, confidentiality breaches) may result in immediate removal without warning.

Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness behavior that violates these guidelines:

Report to Squad Captain

For minor issues, start with your Squad Captain who can mediate and resolve conflicts.

Contact Support

Email: report@foundersfleet.com
Include screenshots, dates, and specific details

Emergency Situations

For threats or illegal activity, contact local authorities first, then notify us immediately.

All reports are confidential. We investigate thoroughly and take appropriate action to maintain community safety.
