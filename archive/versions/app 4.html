EconoMaestro

Read the Economic Room—Without an Econ Degree

EconoMaestro is your macro signal filter. It analyzes public forecasts, scores each economist's accuracy over time, and delivers credibility-weighted insights that help you:

  • ✓ Time strategic decisions like fundraising, hiring, or market entry
  • ✓ Understand which macro signals matter most right now
  • ✓ See through noise and narratives with data-backed clarity

Think of it like Rotten Tomatoes for economic commentary—only now, you get the director's cut.

Key Features

✅ Smart Take Index

The top trending insights from macro analysts, filtered by credibility—not clickbait.

CredScore™

See how often a forecaster has nailed their calls. Powered by a scoring algorithm that evaluates:

  • • Prediction precision
  • • Time horizon realism
  • • Signal vs. volatility
📬 Forecast Accuracy Fridays

A weekly digest emailed to your inbox featuring:

  • • Top 5 most accurate takes of the week
  • • Worst predictions (aka "Cold Streaks")
  • • Founder-specific signals (e.g. "now's a good time to raise if you're B2B SaaS")

How It Works

🧹

Ingests Forecasts

We scan public commentary from central banks, analysts, substack economists, and market reports.

Applies the Scoring Engine

Every forecast gets compared against real-world macro indicators like CPI, unemployment, and interest rates.

Surfaces Actionable Signals

We show founders what matters now—with clarity, confidence, and context.
