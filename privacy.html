Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 31, 2025

Introduction

FoundersFleet ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform for founder collaboration and squad-based accountability.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Name and email address
  • LinkedIn profile information (if you choose to connect)
  • Company/startup information
  • Location (city/state)
  • Industry and startup stage
  • Skills and expertise
  • Phone number (if you opt-in to SMS notifications)

Usage Information

We automatically collect:

  • Log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited)
  • Device information
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies
  • Squad activity and participation metrics
  • Mission completion and progress data

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Match you with compatible founders for squad formation
  • Facilitate squad meetings and collaboration
  • Track and display your progress on objectives and missions
  • Send notifications about squad activities and meetings
  • Improve our matching algorithms and platform features
  • Communicate important updates and changes
  • Ensure platform safety and prevent abuse

Information Sharing

With Squad Members

Your profile information, objectives, and progress are visible to members of your squad(s) to facilitate collaboration and accountability.

With Other Founders

Your public profile (name, company, bio, skills) may be visible to other founders on the platform, subject to your privacy settings.

Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers who help us operate the platform
  • Analytics providers to improve our services
  • Law enforcement when required by law

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and download your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Control your profile visibility settings

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required for legal purposes.

Children's Privacy

FoundersFleet is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or platform notification.

Contact Us
